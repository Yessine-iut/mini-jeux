Rules

Goal to the game

Your objective is to have the required number of points before you run out of moves.

Easy : 3500 pts, 30 moves
Medium : 3500 pts, 25 moves
Hard : 4500 pts, 20 moves

How to earn points

  • Match-3

    You earn points when you line up three cookies of the same type, we call it a match-3

    Cookie Pts

  • Quest

    You can earn points by completing quests, the quests become more and more difficult depending on the difficulty level chosen
